About

HelpEachOther is India’s first peer-to-peer resource sharing platform. It was started in May 2021 during the on-set of the COVID-19 lockdown in India. It brings the concept of helping your neighbour at a time of need – to a virtual space. Users can connect with others in their community by placing requests for essential resources.
